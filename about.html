Basthi Ki Patashala

About Us

Basthi Ki Patashala is a grassroots non-profit initiative that brings free education, moral values, and cultural learning to children in underserved communities, especially slums ("basthis"). Founded by a team of passionate educators and social workers, our goal is to empower the next generation with wisdom, confidence, and compassion.

Through evening and weekend classes, we help children develop not just literacy skills, but also leadership, discipline, and empathy. Our unique curriculum blends modern learning with timeless values — including storytelling, yoga, devotional music, arts, hygiene awareness, and community service.

We believe that true upliftment begins where it's needed most — at the grassroots. Every child, regardless of background, deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and dream big.

With the help of our selfless volunteers, Basthi Ki Patashala now runs learning programs across multiple urban communities. And we're just getting started.
